## The SEC Lawsuit -- The Defining Event

This is the case that shaped crypto regulation in the US. The legal team lived this for 5 years.

### Timeline

| Date | Event |
|---|---|
| **Dec 2020** | SEC charges Ripple + executives with $1.3B unregistered securities offering |
| **2021-2022** | Discovery, motions, Howey test arguments |
| **Jul 2023** | Judge Torres rules: XRP on public exchanges is NOT a security. Institutional sales ARE securities violations. |
| **Aug 2025** | Settlement: $125M penalty (SEC originally wanted $2B). Executives cleared. Both sides drop appeals. |
| **Mar 2026** | SEC-CFTC joint framework classifies XRP as a **digital commodity** -- oversight moves to CFTC |
| **Apr 2026** | Case fully closed, no pending appeals |

### What This Means in the Room

- The legal team fought the most consequential crypto case in US history and won (mostly)
- They have strong opinions about securities law, the Howey test, and regulatory overreach
- Stuart Alderoty (CLO) was the public face of the fight -- he's well-known in crypto legal circles
- They'll likely view AI through the lens of "can this help us never go through that again"

### MiCA (EU) -- Deadline Looming

MiCA's transitional period for existing Crypto Asset Service Providers ends **July 1, 2026**. Ripple's legal team is almost certainly deep in MiCA compliance work right now. The Luxembourg EMI license is their beachhead.

### National Trust Bank Charter

Conditional OCC approval (effective April 2026) lets Ripple offer digital asset custody under federal oversight. This is a big deal -- it means Ripple's custody business operates under bank-level regulation, not just state MTLs.

### What NOT to Say

- Don't call XRP a cryptocurrency "like Bitcoin" -- they'll correct you. XRP is a digital commodity with specific utility in cross-border payments.
- Don't assume the SEC case was a loss -- they view it as a landmark win (XRP is not a security on exchanges).
- Don't oversimplify their regulatory burden -- this is one of the most heavily regulated crypto companies in existence, by choice.
